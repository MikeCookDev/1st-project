A Tribute to Becky Lynch

Straight Fire!

Rebecca Quin, better known as Becky Lynch, is an Irish professional wrestler and actress.
She is currently signed to WWE, where she is a member of the SmackDown brand.
She began wrestling in 2002, in 2006 she suffered a head injury and was diagnosed with possible
damage to her eighth cranial nerve, which nearly ended her career.
Becky returned to wrestling as a manger in 2011, and in 2013 she was signed to a contract to WWE.
Not only has she become one of the most beloved wrestlers on the roster, she has also made history
by becoming the inaugural Smackdown Womens champion in 2016!

    Championships and Accomplishments

  • Ranked No. 4 of the top 50 female wrestlers in the PWI Female 50 in 2016
  • World Queens of Chaos Championship (1 time)
  • SuperGirls Championship (1 time)
  • Winner SuperGirls Championship tournament (2005)
  • WWE SmackDown Women's Championship (1 time, inaugural)
